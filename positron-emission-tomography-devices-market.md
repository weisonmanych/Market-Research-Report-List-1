<p><p>The Positron Emission Tomography (PET) devices market comprises two primary types: full ring PET scanners and partial ring PET scanners. Full ring PET scanners feature a complete circular array of detectors, offering enhanced image resolution and sensitivity for detailed functional imaging. In contrast, partial ring PET scanners consist of fewer detectors, resulting in a compact design suitable for specific applications. Both types cater to varying diagnostic needs and are increasingly adopted in clinical settings for precise imaging in oncology, cardiology, and neurology.</p></p>
