<p><ul><li>Double-Acting Piston Seals</li><li>Single-Acting Piston Seals</li><li>Custom Designed Piston Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piston seals market includes three primary types: double-acting piston seals, single-acting piston seals, and custom-designed piston seals. Double-acting seals enable pressure sealing on both sides, making them ideal for applications requiring bidirectional movement. Single-acting seals provide sealing on one side, suitable for simpler systems. Custom-designed piston seals are tailored to specific requirements, accommodating unique dimensions, materials, and performance needs. Together, these seal types cater to diverse industrial applications, ensuring efficient operation and minimizing leakage.</p></p>
