<p><p>Metallised Polypropylene Films (MPP Films) are specialized films made by coating a polypropylene substrate with a thin layer of metal, usually aluminum. MPP Films are widely used in various sectors, including food packaging, consumer goods, and pharmaceuticals, owing to their light weight, durability, and aesthetic appeal.</p><p>The Metallised Polypropylene Films (MPP Films) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
