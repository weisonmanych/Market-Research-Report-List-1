<p><p>The Semiconductor Metrology and Inspection Equipment market consists of key types such as Optical and E-Beam technologies. Optical systems utilize light to measure and inspect semiconductor wafers, providing rapid data and high-resolution imaging for critical parameters. E-Beam equipment, on the other hand, uses electron beams for detailed analysis, allowing for higher resolution and precision but typically at slower speeds. Together, these technologies play essential roles in ensuring quality and performance in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
