<p><p>The hair mask market features two main types: steam-free hair masks and thermal steam hair masks. Steam-free hair masks provide intensive conditioning and repair without the use of heat, making them easy to apply and suitable for all hair types. Conversely, thermal steam hair masks utilize heat to enhance absorption of nutrients, promoting deeper penetration into the hair shaft. This method often leads to more noticeable results, making thermal masks popular among those seeking intensive treatment for damaged hair.</p></p>
