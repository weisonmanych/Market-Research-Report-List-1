<p><p>The FR-4 PCB market consists of three primary types: single-sided, double-sided, and multilayer PCBs. Single-sided FR-4 PCBs have components on one side, suitable for simpler applications. Double-sided FR-4 PCBs feature components on both sides, enhancing density and functionality, making them ideal for more complex devices. Multilayer FR-4 PCBs consist of multiple layers, enabling intricate designs for advanced electronics, supporting high-density interconnections and improved performance. Each type caters to varying complexity, size, and application needs across industries.</p></p>
