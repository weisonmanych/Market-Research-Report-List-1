<p><p>Micro welding equipment encompasses various technologies, primarily including pulsed arc welding machines and resistance welding machines. Pulsed arc welding machines use controlled bursts of energy to join small components with precision, ideal for delicate tasks in electronics and jewelry. Resistance welding machines employ electrical resistance for localized heating, efficiently joining metals through pressure, often used in automotive and aerospace applications. Additionally, other micro welding technologies encompass laser welding and ultrasonic welding, catering to specialized applications in diverse industries.</p></p>
