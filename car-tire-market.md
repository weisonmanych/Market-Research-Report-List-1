<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature cord layers that run at right angles to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, increased durability, and improved handling. They dominate the market due to advanced technology and performance benefits. In contrast, bias tires have cords that crisscross at angles, providing a more robust construction suitable for certain applications like off-road vehicles, but they tend to have shorter lifespans and lower performance on highways.</p></p>
