<p><p>Cold water soluble creamers are categorized into two main types: dairy creamers and non-dairy creamers. Dairy creamers are made from milk or dairy derivatives, offering a rich, creamy taste and nutritional benefits such as protein and calcium. In contrast, non-dairy creamers are typically plant-based, utilizing ingredients like coconut oil or soy, and are designed for lactose-intolerant consumers. Both types are formulated for easy dissolution in cold beverages, catering to a growing demand for convenient and diverse beverage options.</p></p>
