<p><ul><li>Single Screw Volumetric Feeder</li><li>Twin Screw Volumetric Feeder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volumetric dosing systems are essential for accurately dispensing bulk materials in various industries. Single screw volumetric feeders use a single screw mechanism to move materials consistently, suitable for powders and granules. In contrast, twin screw volumetric feeders employ two intermeshing screws for improved material handling, ensuring better mixing and feeding of more challenging materials, including those with varying densities. Both systems enhance efficiency and precision in production processes, catering to distinct material characteristics and process requirements.</p></p>
