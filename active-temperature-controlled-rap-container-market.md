<p><p>Active temperature controlled RAP (Rapid Agriculture Packaging) containers utilize advanced insulation methods to maintain desired temperatures during transport. Two primary types are Phase Change Materials (PCM) and Vacuum Insulation Panels (VIPs). PCMs absorb or release heat as they change phases, ensuring stable temperatures for perishable goods. VIPs feature an evacuated core that minimizes heat transfer, offering superior thermal efficiency. Together, these technologies enhance product longevity and quality by providing reliable temperature control for sensitive agricultural and pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
