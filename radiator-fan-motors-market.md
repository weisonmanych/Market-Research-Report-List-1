<p><strong>The Radiator Fan Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brush Type</li><li>Brushless Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiator fan motors market is primarily divided into brush type and brushless type motors. Brush type motors use carbon brushes for commutation, offering straightforward design and lower cost but higher maintenance due to brush wear. Conversely, brushless type motors utilize electronic commutation, resulting in higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and reduced noise. Although brushless motors are often more expensive, their growing popularity is driven by advancements in technology and increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions in automotive applications.</p></p>
