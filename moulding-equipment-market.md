<p><p>The moulding equipment market encompasses various types tailored for different manufacturing processes. Vertical moulding equipment is designed for efficient production in limited space, ideal for large parts. Flask moulding equipment employs reusable frames to create sand moulds, enhancing productivity and reducing costs in metal casting. Match moulding equipment uses a dual-system approach to produce high-quality moulds with precision, promising reduced turnaround time and improved surface finish. Each type serves unique applications, driving advancements in casting technologies.</p></p>
