<p><ul><li>Conventional Oil</li><li>Full-synthetic Oil</li><li>Synthetic-blend Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car motor oil market consists of three main types: conventional oil, full-synthetic oil, and synthetic-blend oil. Conventional oil, derived from crude oil, offers basic lubrication and protection at a lower cost but requires more frequent changes. Full-synthetic oil, made from chemically engineered compounds, provides superior performance, better protection, and longer intervals between changes, making it ideal for high-performance engines. Synthetic-blend oil combines both types, offering enhanced protection at a more affordable price, catering to drivers seeking a balance between cost and performance.</p></p>
