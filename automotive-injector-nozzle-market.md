<p><strong>The Automotive Injector Nozzle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SFI</li><li>GDI</li><li>SFI+GDI</li><li>DDI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive injector nozzle market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **SFI (Sequential Fuel Injection)**: Injects fuel into the intake manifold in sync with engine cycles, enhancing efficiency.</p><p>2. **GDI (Gasoline Direct Injection)**: Delivers fuel directly into the combustion chamber, optimizing performance and emissions.</p><p>3. **SFI+GDI**: Combines both systems to leverage the advantages of each for improved fuel economy and power output.</p><p>4. **DDI (Diesel Direct Injection)**: Injects diesel fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions in diesel engines. </p><p>Each type is designed for specific engine requirements and performance outcomes.</p></p>
