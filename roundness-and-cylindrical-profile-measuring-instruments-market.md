<p><ul><li>Sensor Rotary</li><li>Worktable Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roundness and cylindrical profile measuring instruments market is characterized by two primary types: sensor rotary and worktable rotary systems. Sensor rotary instruments utilize high-precision sensors mounted on a rotating fixture to measure the roundness of cylindrical objects, providing detailed data on surface irregularities. In contrast, worktable rotary systems rotate the workpiece while the measuring apparatus remains stationary, enabling comprehensive profiling of cylindrical features. Both types are essential for ensuring quality control in manufacturing processes, particularly in automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
