<p><strong>The Bodyboards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE</li><li>PP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bodyboard market is primarily divided into two types based on materials: PE (polyethylene) and PP (polypropylene). PE bodyboards are softer, providing a more forgiving ride, making them suitable for beginners and casual users. They offer great durability against impacts. In contrast, PP bodyboards are stiffer and lighter, enhancing speed and performance, which are favored by more experienced riders. Each type caters to different preferences and skill levels, influencing consumer choices in the market.</p></p>
