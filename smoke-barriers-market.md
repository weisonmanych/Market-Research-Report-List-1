<p><p>The smoke barriers market is segmented into Active and Passive types. In contrast, Passive smoke barriers are permanent structures made from fire-resistant materials, designed to prevent smoke spread without mechanical intervention. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing fire safety within buildings, ensuring compliance with regulations while protecting occupants and property from smoke hazards.</p></p>
