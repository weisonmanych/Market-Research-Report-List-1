<p><strong>The Soil Compactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Rolling Compactor</li><li>Impact Compactor</li><li>Vibrating Compactor</li><li>Compound Action Compactor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil compactors market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Static Rolling Compactor** - utilizes weight and static load to compact soil, ideal for granular materials.</p><p>2. **Impact Compactor** - uses a heavy weight that drops repeatedly to increase density, suited for cohesive soils.</p><p>3. **Vibrating Compactor** - employs vibrations to enhance soil particles' interaction, effective for various soil types.</p><p>4. **Compound Action Compactor** - combines static, impact, and vibrating actions for versatile applications, achieving optimal compaction in diverse conditions.</p></p>
