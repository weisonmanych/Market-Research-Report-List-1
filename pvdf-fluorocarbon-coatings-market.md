<p><ul><li>Powder Coating</li><li>Solvent Coating</li><li>Waterborne Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) fluorocarbon coatings are categorized into three main types: powder coatings, solvent coatings, and waterborne coatings. Powder coatings, known for their durability and environmental benefits, are applied as a dry powder that cures into a hard finish. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents for application, offering smooth finishes but posing environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. Waterborne coatings, combining the ease of water-based application with lower environmental impact, provide a safer alternative while maintaining good performance characteristics.</p></p>
