<p><p>The Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) system market is categorized by different sensing technologies: Camera, Fusion, LiDAR, and Radar. Camera-based systems use visual data to detect obstacles and lane markings, while Fusion combines inputs from multiple sensors for enhanced accuracy and reliability. LiDAR employs laser technology to create 3D maps of the environment, offering precise object detection. Radar uses radio waves to measure distance and speed, effectively functioning in various weather conditions. Each type plays a crucial role in vehicle safety advancements.</p></p>
