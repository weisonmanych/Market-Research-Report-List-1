<p><p>The femoral venous catheter market includes two primary types: unipolar and bipolar femoral arterial cannulae. Unipolar femoral arterial cannulae are designed for single-direction blood flow, providing a straightforward approach for vascular access. In contrast, bipolar femoral arterial cannulae allow for bidirectional flow, enhancing the efficiency of fluid management and reducing the risk of complications during procedures. Both types are critical in various medical settings, including surgeries and critical care, facilitating effective venous access and monitoring.</p></p>
