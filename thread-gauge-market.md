<p><p>The thread gauge market is comprised of two primary types: thread plug gauges and thread ring gauges. Thread plug gauges are used to check the internal threads of holes, ensuring they meet specified tolerances. In contrast, thread ring gauges assess external threads of components, verifying their dimensional accuracy. Both types are essential for quality control in manufacturing industries, ensuring threaded parts fit properly and function as intended, thereby reducing the risk of assembly failures and enhancing overall product reliability.</p></p>
