<p><p>The power lawn mower market is divided into two main types: wireless and wired mowers. Wireless lawn mowers are powered by batteries, offering greater mobility and eliminating the hassle of cords, making them ideal for larger yards. They are often quieter and more environmentally friendly. In contrast, wired mowers rely on electricity from a power outlet, providing continuous power without the need for recharging. However, their range is limited by cord length, which can restrict maneuverability in larger spaces.</p></p>
