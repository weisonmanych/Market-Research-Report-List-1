<p><p>The Portable X-Ray Equipment for security purposes market includes two main types: Computed Radiography (CR) Equipment and Digital Radiography (DR) Equipment. CR Equipment uses phosphorus imaging plates to capture X-ray images, requiring additional processing, making it more portable. In contrast, DR Equipment employs digital sensors for immediate image display, enhancing efficiency and image quality. Both types are essential for security applications such as airport screenings, border control, and critical infrastructure protection, ensuring thorough inspections with minimal disruption.</p></p>
