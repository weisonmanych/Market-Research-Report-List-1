<p><p>The ureteroscopy market consists of two primary types: single-use ureteroscopes and reusable ureteroscopes. Single-use ureteroscopes are designed for one-time use, minimizing the risk of infections and eliminating the need for sterilization. These scopes are particularly advantageous in high-turnover environments. In contrast, reusable ureteroscopes are durable instruments that can be sterilized and utilized multiple times, offering cost-effectiveness over the long term. Both types cater to different surgical needs and preferences, influencing adoption rates in healthcare settings.</p></p>
