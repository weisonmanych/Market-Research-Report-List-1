<p><ul><li>Syringe Pump</li><li>Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Pressure Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microfluidic pumps market comprises three main types: syringe pumps, peristaltic pumps, and pressure pumps. Syringe pumps provide precise and consistent fluid delivery through a syringe mechanism, ideal for applications requiring accuracy. Peristaltic pumps operate by compressing a flexible tube, ensuring contamination-free fluid movement and making them suitable for biological and chemical processes. Pressure pumps utilize pressure differentials to drive fluid flow, offering versatility for various microfluidic applications. Each type serves unique needs in research, diagnostics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
