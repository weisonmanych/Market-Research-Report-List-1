<p><strong>The Differential ADC Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-ended</li><li>Pseudo-differential</li><li>Fully-differential</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Differential ADC driver market is segmented into three main types: single-ended, pseudo-differential, and fully-differential. Single-ended drivers operate with one input and ground reference, typically offering simplicity and low cost. Pseudo-differential drivers enhance noise immunity by using two inputs but may still reference ground. Fully-differential drivers provide improved performance by using two complementary signals, minimizing common-mode noise and enhancing dynamic range, making them ideal for high-precision applications. Each type serves specific needs in various industries, from consumer electronics to industrial systems.</p></p>
