<p><p>Hydrogen fuel cells for boats primarily include Polymer Electrolyte Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC) and Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC). PEMFCs operate at low temperatures and are suitable for marine applications due to their quick startup and high power density, making them ideal for smaller vessels. Meanwhile, SOFCs function at higher temperatures and are more efficient for larger ships, providing long operational durations. Both types promote sustainable maritime energy solutions, reducing emissions and enhancing energy efficiency in the boating industry.</p></p>
