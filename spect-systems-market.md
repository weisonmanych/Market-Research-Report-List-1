<p><ul><li>Hybrid SPECT</li><li>Standalone SPECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SPECT systems are categorized into two main market types: hybrid SPECT and standalone SPECT. Hybrid SPECT integrates single-photon emission computed tomography with other imaging modalities, such as CT or MRI, providing comprehensive diagnostic information through enhanced anatomical and functional imaging. Standalone SPECT systems, on the other hand, focus solely on producing high-quality functional imaging without additional modalities, making them more cost-effective for specific applications. Both types play crucial roles in medical diagnostics, particularly in neurology and oncology.</p></p>
